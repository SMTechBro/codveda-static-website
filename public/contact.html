COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked

Quick answers to things most clients want to know before getting started.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary by scope. A standard web app takes 8-12 weeks, while complex enterprise platforms can take 4-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline in our initial proposal.

Do you sign NDAs?

Absolutely. We prioritize client confidentiality. We are happy to sign your standard NDA or provide our own before discussing any proprietary project details.

What does the onboarding process look like?

Onboarding begins with a strategy kick-off call, followed by access to our project management tools and the introduction of your dedicated lead developer.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages that include security updates, bug fixes, and performance optimizations to keep your product running smoothly.

Can you work with our existing tech stack?

We are experts in most modern frameworks (React, Node, Python, AWS). We can audit your current stack and either integrate directly or suggest improvements.
